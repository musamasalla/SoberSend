Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

SoberSend ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by Musa Masalla. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Contact names you choose to lock. These are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and are never transmitted to any server.

App selections. The apps you choose to block are managed entirely by Apple's Screen Time (FamilyControls) framework. We never see which apps you select — Apple handles this privately on-device.

Challenge attempt logs. Records of challenge attempts (pass/fail, timestamp, contact or app name) are stored locally on your device for the Morning Report feature.

Sober notes & intentions. Any notes you write are stored locally on your device.

Schedule preferences. Your lockdown schedule (start time, end time, active days) is stored locally.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics, usage tracking, or telemetry.
  • We do not access your messages, calls, photos, or browsing history.
  • We do not store any data on external servers.
  • We do not use third-party SDKs that collect user data.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

3. Speech Recognition

The tongue twister challenge uses Apple's on-device Speech Recognition framework. Audio is processed locally and is not sent to any server. Microphone access is only active during an active challenge session.

4. Biometric Authentication

Emergency Unlock uses Face ID or Touch ID via Apple's LocalAuthentication framework. Biometric data never leaves your device and is managed entirely by iOS.

5. In-App Purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

6. Notifications

We send local notifications (morning reports, challenge prompts). These are generated on your device and are not push notifications from a server.

7. Children's Privacy

SoberSend is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Data Deletion

All data is stored on your device. Deleting the app removes all data. You can also reset your data within the app's settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
