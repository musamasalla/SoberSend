1. Description of Service

SoberSend is a personal accountability tool that helps you avoid sending regrettable messages and accessing certain apps during designated hours. It uses Apple's Screen Time (FamilyControls) framework to block apps and provides sobriety challenges to unlock them.

SoberSend is not a medical device and does not provide medical, psychological, or professional advice. It is a personal accountability tool only. If you are struggling with substance abuse, please contact a medical professional or call SAMHSA at 1-800-662-4357.

3. Accuracy of Challenges

The sobriety challenges (math, memory, speech) are designed as deterrents, not as scientifically validated sobriety tests. Results may not accurately reflect your level of impairment. Do not rely on passing a challenge as proof of sobriety.

5. Free Tier

The free tier allows locking 1 contact and 1 app, with Easy and Medium challenge difficulty. Premium unlocks unlimited contacts, unlimited apps, all difficulty levels, full statistics, and shareable morning reports.

6. Contact Blocking Limitations

SoberSend cannot block iMessage or SMS contacts directly. Apple does not provide APIs for third-party apps to intercept or block messages. The "Locked Contacts" feature is an accountability tracker — it records who you should avoid messaging and requires a challenge to remove a contact from the locked list.

7. Emergency Unlock

The Emergency Unlock feature bypasses all locks for 5 minutes and has a 24-hour cooldown. This feature requires biometric authentication (Face ID or passcode). It is intended for genuine emergencies only.
